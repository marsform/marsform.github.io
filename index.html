About MetaFarm
MetaFarm is a cryptoproject, a metaverse that combines virtual and real farms. Real farms will be located in the territory of 400 hectares in Moldova. All player actions at the gaming platform entail corresponding changes in the real farm. You can just play for fun or set a goal and establish a large virtual agricultural business.

MEDIA

We made a comprehensive market analysis and selected the technologies most suitable for the project in terms of scaling security.

We opted for Polygon.

Polygon PoS is a scalable Tier 2 solution that provides unprecedented transaction speed and cost savings through the use of side-chain for transaction processing. PoS provides asset security with a reliable Plasma bridging framework and a decentralized network of PoS validators.
